FAQ
Does higher RTP mean I will win?

No. RTP is a long-run average. It does not predict short sessions.

What does “high volatility” actually mean?

Bigger swings. Longer dry spells, bigger spikes. It’s about variance, not RTP.

Can a casino change RTP?

Some games have multiple RTP configurations. The specific version matters.
