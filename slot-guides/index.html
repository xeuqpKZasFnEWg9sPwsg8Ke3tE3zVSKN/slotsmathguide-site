FAQ
Does higher RTP mean I will win?

No. RTP is a long-run average across huge numbers of spins. In a normal session, volatility dominates, so results can look nothing like RTP.

What does “high volatility” actually mean?

It means a wider spread of outcomes. Expect longer downswings and occasional big spikes. Two slots can share RTP and still feel completely different.

Can a casino change RTP?

Some games ship with multiple RTP configurations. Casinos pick which version to offer, so the exact RTP can vary. (The RTP guide explains how to think about this.)
