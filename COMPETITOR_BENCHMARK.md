# Competitor benchmark (UX patterns to exceed)

Benchmarked (B2B affiliate platform sites):
- iRev partner platform: https://irev.com/partner-platform/
- PartnerMatrix: https://partnermatrix.com/
- Affilka: https://affilka.com/

## What they do well (patterns)
- Clear, scannable value props (hero + bullets)
- Strong information hierarchy (feature sections with short lists)
- Trust & security signals (KYC/2FA/logs/compliance)
- “Proof” sections (stats, client logos, testimonials)
- Consistent component system (cards, callouts, comparison blocks)

## How we’ll beat them (for a consumer publication)
- Faster pages (static, no heavy JS)
- Better readability (typography, TOC, callouts)
- Better intent matching (help queries + hub architecture)
- Stronger transparency (methodology + last-updated + evidence-first rules)
- Ontario-first compliance positioning (19+, regulated framing)
