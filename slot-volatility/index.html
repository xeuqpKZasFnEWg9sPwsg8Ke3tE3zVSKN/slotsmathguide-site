Slot Volatility (Variance)

Volatility is how “swingy” a slot feels. Two games can share RTP but have very different volatility.

Low
Frequent small hits
Smoother balance changes.
High
Dry spells + spikes
Longer losing streaks, bigger wins.
Trap
Short-session myths
“Hot/cold” is not evidence.
