Responsible Gambling

Gambling is entertainment, not a way to make money. You can lose money. If you choose to gamble:

  • Set a budget you can afford to lose and stick to it.
  • Set time limits and take breaks.
  • Don’t chase losses.
  • Avoid gambling when stressed, upset, or under the influence.

Age & legality: Only gamble if you meet the minimum age requirement (often 18+ or 21+) and it is legal where you are.

If gambling stops being fun or feels hard to control, consider using safer gambling tools (deposit limits, time-outs, self-exclusion) and seeking support services in your area.
