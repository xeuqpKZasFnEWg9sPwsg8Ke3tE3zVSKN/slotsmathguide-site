Processing vs Sent vs Received (Withdrawal Status)

Most confusion happens because “pending” can mean different stages. This page gives you the exact question to ask support and what timelines apply at each step.

Stage 1
Processing
Casino review, KYC, bonus checks. This is usually the bottleneck.
Stage 2
Sent
Casino released the payment. Now the rail timing applies (Interac/e-wallet/crypto).
Stage 3
Received
Funds available in your account/wallet. If not, it’s usually method mismatch or holds.
Copy/paste support message
“Can you confirm whether my withdrawal is still processing or has been sent? If it’s processing, what is the exact hold reason (KYC doc, bonus review, method match, manual review) and what do you need from me to clear it?”

FAQ

What’s the fastest way to avoid bonus cashout surprises?
Read the wagering requirement, max cashout, game contribution rules, and withdrawal limits before you deposit. If anything is unclear, don’t accept the bonus.
Do withdrawal times differ by payment method?
Yes. Processing time depends on the operator’s review + the method (e.g., e-wallets often faster than cards). Some methods also have higher failure rates or extra verification.
Why do casinos ask for verification (KYC)?
To prevent fraud, comply with regulation, and confirm identity. Expect it before first withdrawal, and sometimes after big wins or method changes.
