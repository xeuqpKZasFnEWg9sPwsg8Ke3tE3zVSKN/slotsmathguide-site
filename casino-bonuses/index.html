Casino Bonuses

Bonuses are not “free money”, they are contracts. If you want to maximize value (and avoid withdrawal surprises), the terms matter more than the headline %.

Most important
Wagering requirements
Bonus-only vs deposit+bonus changes everything.
Often missed
Max bet
Break it and winnings can be voided.
Hidden cap
Max cashout
Limits what you can withdraw even if you win big.
How to evaluate a bonus in 60 seconds
  1. Check what wagering multiplies (bonus only vs deposit+bonus).
  2. Check max bet and max cashout.
  3. Check game contribution and restricted games.
  4. Check time limit and withdrawal method requirements.
Common traps
  • Huge match % with deposit+bonus wagering.
  • Low max cashout (e.g., “5x bonus”).
  • Slots contribute 10–20% while tables contribute 0%.
  • Strict max bet that is easy to break.

FAQ
Should I always take the bonus?

No. If you want fast withdrawals, a bonus can make things slower or impossible until requirements are met.

Why do casinos limit max bet on bonuses?

To reduce the chance of a single big win clearing wagering too quickly.

What’s the safest “simple” bonus?

Bonus-only wagering, reasonable max bet, no low max cashout, and clear contribution rules.
